The City
Citywide QR Code Non-linear Narrative Scavenger Hunt | 2014

The City's narrative centers on a fictional city on a fictional planet floating in the outer edges of the universe. Once a colonized boom town, the city's technology and major export have become obsolete, rendering the colony useless, the handful of immigrants and displaced miners on it stranded. Presented at the Rochester Fringe Festival in 2014.
