shonen book
Live Glitch Performance | 2022 - ongoing

shonen book is a live glitch, analogue and digital performance duo consisting of me and evan bobrow. Our process involves analogue video mixing/corrupting, anime VHS tapes, live video game playing, and tons of distortion pedals. Through this breaking of the screen, a queer inscription is formed, an undoing of "legacy" or violence, or something akin to the power of gay "fanfiction."


As always, collaboration helps direct my practice outside, and the shonen book process is mostly improvised, poetry-dance-play.

shonen book has performed several times, including for the Rochester Experimental Music Week (2022).
