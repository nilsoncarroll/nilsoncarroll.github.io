NES Glitches 1
NES glitch compilation | 2020

I started performing and recording live NES glitches in the VSW auditorium in November 2019. I was immediately struck by how each game behaved radically different as I altered at random hundreds of hex values within the game's RAM. The initial NES glitches are mostly from the Dragon Quest games, Gargoyle's Quest 2, Bomberman 2,


By breaking open (ruining) the systems and metaphors these games are designed to operate with, a certain queerness arises.


Installation at Rochester Contemporary Art Center, 2020
