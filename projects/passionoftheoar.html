Passion of the Oar
13:23 | 16mm film | 2017

A collaborative 16mm film between Mary Lew, Megan Amedan and I - a narrative about the nature of longing and the ecstasy of possibility. An overt gnostic quest for love subverted through anachronism. Watch here with a special soundtrack from Ian Downey.
