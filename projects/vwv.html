VWV
Expanded Cinema Performance | 2017 - ongoing

VWV is an ongoing expanded cinema performance project with Mary Lew, Tara Nelson, and me featuring multiple projections, appropriated and looped 16mm films, optical tricks, and other sense-spells.


We have performed for Movies with Live Soundtracks, which featured audio performances by local Rochester noise bands Pengo, Jungle Heart, and No (of Carbon Records).

"When we say expanded cinema we actually mean expanded consciousness. Expanded cinema does not mean computer films, video phosphors, atomic light, or spherical projections. Expanded cinema isn't a movie at all."

- Gene Youngblood, Expanded Cinema
