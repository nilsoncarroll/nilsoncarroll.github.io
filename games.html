Pyramid of Faggots
NES ROM Hack | 2016

A ROM hack made with the original creator of Dragon Pervert, Tettsui77. The player is locked in the first room of Dragon Quest. The hack subverts the Dragon Quest formula. The player’s identity as a chosen hero of legend is put into question, the traditional roles of the king and kidnapped princess are revoked.

The player cannot even enter the adventure-myth to restore the binary balance of good versus evil because the exit of the first room has been erased. A certain disjointed beauty arises from the emphasis on mistranslation.

Sex and sexuality become a blurred system that replaces the imperialist system of violent exchanges. There is a parallel between the king and his knights proclaiming the player’s character as the Legendary Faggot and being a kid and getting called a faggot at school.

 
 
GraveCamp
RPG | 2015

GraveCamp is a JRPG built for a one month game jam. It’s a game about bodies and the world being made of the same materials. Download on itch.

Video games are made up of worlds that are highly designed, rigid digital hellworlds most often designed to kill the player in abstract ways. These aspects of gameplay and game-worlds act as a kind of gnostic metaphor for the player to enact and try to resolve.

Gnostic qualities implicit in games include bodies as vessels for magic, the physical manifestations of souls and their relationship to bodies, multiple souls inhabiting a single body, the esoteric nature of manipulating the physical world in order to gain metaphysical points, hierarchies of natural elements, abilities to transform and reset one’s physical and mental self, and the idea that a created world inherently houses an evil power in its depths.

 
 
The City
Citywide QR Code Non-linear Narrative Scavenger Hunt | 2014

The City's narrative centers on a fictional city on a fictional planet floating in the outer edges of the universe. Once a colonized boom town, the city's technology and major export have become obsolete, rendering the colony useless, the handful of immigrants and displaced miners on it stranded. Presented at the Rochester Fringe Festival in 2014.
